Some Info...

Start private room by following link from the main page and share the link  with anyone you would like to join.  Your private room name is never made public nor does the server provide a directory of active rooms.

Please note that video feeds are not recorded, are encrypted, and no personally identifiable information is retained. The Videobridge does record your IP Address within the log however, these are subsequently purged every 24 hours to preserve disk space.

Free VPN Service is avaliable to anyone interested unfortunatley this process has not been automated but you if you contact us we will setup access and give you login instructions.
